Ergocure Advisory Board


Dr. Rajshree Malgaonkar, Australia
Experienced Professional Services Manager with a demonstrated history of working in the injury management industry. Having worked in India for several years, she moved to Australia in 2003, Currently working as Professional services manager in a well-known workers’ compensation company, Sydney, Australia. Skilled in coaching & mentoring, case management, ergonomics, occupational therapy specific assessments injury management and subject matter expert. Strong allied health professional background in occupational therapy and a Post-grad Diploma in Rehab with 18 years of clinical experience and over 14 years' experience in injury management - compensable and non-compensable injuries and disabilities including vision and hearing impairment.



Dr. Anita Keswani Bhatt, California, USA
She is an experienced Occupational Therapist, a Certified Ergonomics Specialist and Physical Agent Modalities Specialist with deep knowledge musculoskeletal system. She specialises in onsite ergonomic assessments at homes and offices and recommend customized workspace and work habit modifications. In addition to using the more conventional therapies which include clinical and ergonomic approaches, She coaches her clients on early symptom detection, lifestyle techniques, injury prevention, postural adjustment, and proper body mechanic techniques.
Her passion is to maximize the productivity of one's 'occupational' role in life! She believes everyone can achieve this regardless of age.
After working in India for many years, She Moved to USA in 1993 and started her own company in 2010. Now works in the heart of Silicon Valley or San Francisco Bay Area, operates her company Balance therapy providing homebased rehabilitation and ergonomic solutions through her team of experts.
