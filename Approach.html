Our Approch

We believes that each individual interacts with the workplace context differently. This is highly influenced by his/her own set of strengths & weakness, intellectual, financial, educational, social, familial, professional skills & physical & mental capacities. We focus on the individual by evaluating each of these challenges & devise our strategies accordingly.

The workplace context itself changes by industry & geographic location.
Our team brings together – Rehabilitation consultants, Physiotherapy practitioners, Ergonomic experts, Organisational Design Experts – in the context of the workplace with well-designed programs that address specific industry needs.

For Corporates: You can find more details in the ERGOLIFE® WORKSHOPS: WORKSPACE PLANNING & ASSESSMENT FOR CORPORATES.
For Individuals: Our ERGOLIFE® MIND-BODY-SOUL WORKSHOPS brings in our Therapeutic intervention program with Brain Programming & Spirituality.This programme provides with activities, techniques and perspectives for stress management, increases internal peace, happiness and hence enhances performance and productivity at work too.


About ERGOCURE

Our daily work actions are exposed to awkward postures, exertions, insufficient rest breaks, lifting stuff wrongly & bad lighting – impacting our body. Most of the factors contributing to this are related to workplace design & ergonomics. This is where ERGOCURE® comes in…
